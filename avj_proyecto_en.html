Project's Philosophy

Improve life quality of children through education for the future raise awareness, with the support of major companies with social responsibility in Mexico.

Mission

Provide support for the education of Mexican children of orphanages or foster homes, by learning techniques and recreational activities, with the support of young foreigners interested in spreading values, new customs, new ways of seeing life, contributing to their integral formation.

Objectives

  • Give a positive impact on children
  • Strengthening values ​​and manners of children
  • Educate children about their future
  • Develop their skills in various fields
  • Improve future generations of our society
  • Contribute to their social and academic training
