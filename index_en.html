AIESEC organizes worldwide in its different local comitees in more than 110 countries and territores, social responsability projects, based on actual topics which have impact on society , these proyects pretend to generate a positive impact on society, where they are developed.

Based on a success in India, Aprendamos a Volar Juntos pretends to be an impact project in children who live an unfavorable situation. We invite you to keep reading and also learn more about us. Join us and become part of this change.

Project

Aprendamos a Volar Juntos (AVJ), is a project with the ideal to raise awareness and improve education levels in institutions which take care of orphans and those whose parents can't take care of them for a certain period of time.

The AVJ project is looking for the empathy between both sides participants, children and AIESEC trainees. Let's remember that, many of these children at any moment can feel rejected, lonely and feel that they do not belong to society, thats why they can present a problem in a future, when they grow up and own their own decisions. About us

Instituto Politécnico Nacional Comitee of AIESEC International Asociation is developing a project named "Aprendamos a Volar Juntos" (AVJ), which pretends to impact the life of children who belong to orphanages, so that at their young age these children begin to create interest for their personal develoment, arts, science and begin to discover their habilites and strengths so that they can be routed to a sucessful future.
